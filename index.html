NetAudit

A native macOS app for checking whether the Wi-Fi you just joined is safe, fast, and private — before you do anything sensitive on it.

One-line install:

curl -fsSL https://netaudit.sreeb.dev/install.sh | bash

Or download the DMG and drag NetAudit.app into your Applications folder: NetAudit-0.8.0.dmg (35 MB). First-launch instructions: INSTALL.md.

Source: github.com/sreebalakrishnan/netaudit
